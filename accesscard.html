Access Card

Get an Access Card

Keep your belongings safe and easy to reach. With the CitySpace Access Card (CSAC), you can securely access personal items stored at shelters, storage sites, and partner locations across Brooklyn.

Cardholders also get access to FreeStore, our donation-based hub for everyday essentials, free of charge.

What is the CSAC?

The CSAC is an access card for individuals experiencing homelessness. It serves as your personal key to retrieve your stories items safely, without needing to carry everything with you day-to-day.

*Free Braille access cards available for individuals who are blind or visually impaired.

How It Works

  • Check eligibility and sign up online or in person at any CSAC Help Center—no ID or permanent address required.
  • Receive your Access Card, which includes a secure barcode linked to your unique storage ID.
  • Use your card at any participating storage location to check in and access your stored personal belongings.
  • Lost your card? Stop by a Help Center or call our 24/7 hotline for a fast, free replacement.

Accessibility Services

  • Wheelchair-friendly lockers and storage rooms
  • Easy-to-navigate facility layouts with ramps, elevators, and wide hallways
  • Staff help with lifting or organizing items
  • Assistance for check-in with memory or developmental needs
  • Multilingual staff and translated materials
  • ASL and large-print options available
