About CitySpace

CitySpace is a grassroots community initiative built to support people experiencing housing insecurity. We provide practical services like room access, lockers for belongings, and essential supplies through our FreeStore network—ensuring every person has a place to keep what matters most.

Our Mission

We believe dignity starts with stability. Whether it’s a warm blanket, a safe place to store documents, or a hygiene kit, CitySpace helps people meet their needs without shame or delay. We aim to restore autonomy and respect—one interaction at a time.

How We Work

CitySpace partners with shelters, mutual aid groups, and local volunteers to operate accessible hubs across NYC. Donations from people like you help keep our lockers stocked and our outreach strong. Every contribution goes directly to supporting someone’s next step forward.
