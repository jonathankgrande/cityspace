FreeStore

Drop off what you can. Take what you need. Give freely. Receive with dignity. FreeStore is a donation-powered resource hub for clothing, hygiene products, and everyday essentials.

How It Works

  • No sign-up or ID required to take what you need.
  • Anyone can contribute — just drop items in designated bins or with staff.
  • All donations are inspected and sorted for safety and dignity.
  • Take items as often as needed — no questions asked.
