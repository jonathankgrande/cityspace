Vision & Mission

CitySpace is a New York–based nonprofit dedicated to restoring dignity and stability for people experiencing homelessness by providing secure, accessible storage for personal belongings and distributing essential items through community donations.

We serve individuals who are too often overlooked—those who carry their lives on their backs, facing daily challenges made harder by the lack of a safe place to store their possessions. Without storage, people risk losing their I.D., important documents, medication, or sentimental items, which in turn creates barriers to employment, healthcare, and housing.

Our mission is to relieve immediate burdens, promote self-agency, and open pathways to long-term stability. We do this because we believe that everyone deserves not just shelter, but space—space to protect what matters, to move forward and to be seen as human.
